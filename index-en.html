Why choose China Airlines Virutal?

China Airlines Virtual was established in Taiwan on September 3, 2021. It is a non-profit virtual airline, where we can enjoy stress-free flights in the virtual skies together with fellow enthusiasts. We don’t expect every pilot to possess in-depth knowledge of aviation literature, aerodynamics, navigation, and meteorology. Beginners will be guided step by step into the world of aviation, while experienced pilots are more than welcome to enjoy stress-free flying here. We also organize regular and occasional events, catering to anyone with a passion for flight and a longing to soar through the endless skies. Whether you’re a novice or a veteran, we sincerely invite you to join this big family.

FAQ

  • What is virtual airlines?

    Virtual airlines is a virtual network that simulates real-world airlines, where pilots can fly in the virtual skies and communicate with other pilots and air traffic control.

  • Will I be fired?

    No, unless you violate the rules of this virtual airline.

  • What exactly do I need to do after joining?

    Soar in the virtual skies and follow the local flight regulations.

  • Is there a salary?

    Of course, there’s no salary.
